Chomtong Insight Meditation Center (International)

The Atisundara Foundation is a non-profit organization established in Canada. The Foundation is raising funds for the International Section of the Northern Vipassana Insight Meditation Center in Chomtong, Thailand. The Meditation Center is run by co-directors, Thanat Chindaporn and Kathryn Chindaporn. Expenses are to be paid under a written agreement between Atisundara Foundation and the co-directors. Information about the Meditation Center can be found on its website which contains a description of the meditation practice and biographies of the co-directors.

The Gift of Dhamma Excels All Gifts

The following quote by L.B. Horner provides a readily accessible answer: We of Today, in view of this wonderful gift of Dhamma that has been vouchsafed to us, cannot be merely recipients. We must be givers too. We have had lofty privilege of coming to know something of the Teaching of the Buddha. We live in a Buddha-era. That is at a time when the teachings of a Buddha are still remembered and are of significance. This alone would make it incumbent on us to spread this Teaching of Peace, inner and outer, as far and wide and faithfully as we can.
