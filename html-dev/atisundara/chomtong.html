Atisundara Foundation is collecting funds for the International Section of the Northern Vipassana Insight Meditation Center in Chomtong, Thailand. Expenses are to be paid under a written agreement between the Foundation and co-directors of the Meditation Center, Thanat Chindaporn and Kathryn Chindaporn. Information about the Meditation Center can be found on its website.

Note: If you wish to donate to the meditation center from within Thailand and/or from a Thai bank account, please contact the meditation center directly. Donations may be received by Atisundara Foundation from outside of Thailand only.

The Gift of Dhamma Excels All Gifts

The following quote by L.B. Horner is particularly apt: we of Today, in view of this wonderful gift of Dhamma that has been vouchsafed to us, cannot be merely recipients. We must be givers too. We have had lofty privilege of coming to know something of the Teaching of the Buddha. We live in a Buddha-era. That is at a time when the teachings of a Buddha are still remembered and are of significance. This alone would make it incumbent on us to spread this Teaching of Peace, inner and outer, as far and wide and faithfully as we can.

How Your Donations are Used

The International Section of the Northern Vipassana Insight Meditation Center in Chomtong, Thailand consists of 19 buildings containing two air conditioned meditation halls, a library, 36 residential rooms for meditators and 16 rooms for teachers, training teachers and assistants. The center regularly engages 21 Thai nationals locally to provide contracted services such as cleaning, maintenance and construction. Standard meditation retreats are 21-days for beginniers and 10-days for intermediate/advanced meditators.
