Overview

One Way Path Group Limited ("One Way Path Group") is an international real estate company based in Toronto, Canada committed to building prosperous and peaceful local communities. We build or add to meditation centers and Temples in Canada, Germany and Thailand along with homes for nearby community members. We arrange asset-based loans for socially responsible businesses and funding for early childhood education in priority communities of need.

Vision

The Vision of One Way Path Group is to build prosperous and peaceful local communities. We view prosperous communities as communities where needs of survival are met, making room for spirtual development. We view peaceful communities as communities where its members devote their reources to charity and refrain from killing, stealing, adultry, lies, drugs and alcohol.

Mission

One Way Path achieves its vision by:

  1. builidng and promiting the growth of Therevada Buddhist Temples and meditation centers;
  2. building homes nearby to Buddhist Temples and meditation centers which can be owned by community members;
  3. providing services to socially responsible businesses in the following areas: private real-estate based lending, formation of real estate corporations and limited partnerships, mergers and acquisition of real estate businesses;
  4. funding early childhood education in communities of need including Buddhist education for ordained and non-ordained children and secular education for non-Buddhist children

Projects may be located anywhere in the world based on a balanced assessment of the following:

  1. significant demand for the project in the community;
  2. symbolic significance of the communnity or location;
  3. demonstrated leadership by the community in insight meditation or other Therevada Buddhist education;
  4. the presence of an existing Therevada Buddhist community or demonstration of signifcant interest in Therevada Buddhism within the community;
  5. stable and predictable local government;

Values

One Way Path Group is part of a network of meditation centres and temples run by students of the late Venerable Ajahn Tong Sirimangalo (Ajahn Tong). We operate under the spritual direction of Ajahn Tong, Venerable Ajahn Dhammo Tach Troung of the Khmer Krom Buddhist Temple in Stoney Creek, Ontario, Canada and Ajahn Tong's senior diciples, Thanat Chindaporn and Kathryn Johnston Chindaporn.

One Way Path Group provides an opportunity for socially responsible investing from a Therevada Buddhist perspective. All corporate projects and investments are screened to exclude profit from weapons, alcohol, recreational drugs or pharmaceuticals, gambling and animal proudcts or animal husbandry.

Financing and Sources of Funds

Financing for One Way Path Group projects are generally raised from private sources on a case-by-case basis. Meditation centres are owned privately and their use for meditation retreats is donated by the owner(s). Operational costs for meditation centers are derived from a mix of donations and user fees. Equity and debt financing for home construction is raised on a for-profit basis from individual private investors or companies and homes are sold for profit to Buddhist and non-Buddhist buyers. Funding for early childhood education consists of income derived from One Way Path Group for-profit initiatives or from indvidual private donations made by the owners of One Way Path Group.
