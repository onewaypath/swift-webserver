COMING SOON
The creation of legacy test tokens has been deprecated

The Buddhavipassana Meditation Centre remains closed during the COVID-19 Emergency Period. The centre will re-open based on guidance from the Government of Ontario.
A new dashboard to manage channels (Plus* and Enterprise Grid)

Organization primary owners and designated admins will have a new dashboard for viewing the channels within their Slack workspace. They’ll also be able to take actions on these channels, such as archiving, renaming and other administrative duties.
*This will be available only for Plus plans with corporate exports enabled.
Increasingly customizable Do Not Disturb settings

Users will soon be able to customize their notification preferences for specific days, including setting Do Not Disturb schedules for weekends only.
Marking threads as unread

Users will soon be able to mark threaded replies as "unread", making it easier to come back to key messages in thread, at a later time.

Data residency: London and Sydney (Plus and Enterprise Grid)

Organizations now have the ability to choose where their Slack data is stored at rest. Frankfurt, Paris, Sydney, London and Tokyo data regions are now available.
Pre-built templates now available in Workflow Builder

Check out the new “Templates” tab in Workflow Builder on the desktop app for quick and easy access to customizable workflows.
New ways to manage workflow creation for your team

Org owners and admins can now set a policy that restricts workflow creation and publishing.
Launch a phone call from Slack

Customers with VoIP services can use an app to start a phone call in Slack, leveraging services such as Zoom Phone, Jabber, RingCentral and Dialpad.
View an app’s security details in the App Directory

We’ve added a new tab to app listings where admins can view key security information. That includes compliance details such as HIPAA and FedRAMP and security practices like data-retention policies.
